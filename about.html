About Nurul Quran

"Making the Holy Quran accessible to everyone"

Nurul Quran is a digital platform dedicated to bringing the light of the Holy Quran to your fingertips. Our goal is to provide a seamless, beautiful, and spiritually enriching experience for every seeker of knowledge.

Key Features

Free & Open

100% free to use, no hidden costs or subscriptions.

Offline Support

Access your favorite Surahs even without an internet connection.

114 Surahs

Complete Holy Quran with full text and translations.

Multi-Language

Available in Arabic, Urdu, and English translations.

Audio Recitations

Listen to beautiful recitations from world-renowned Qaris.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the wisdom of the Quran is just a click away for every person on Earth. We strive to maintain the highest standards of accuracy and humility in our work, seeking only the pleasure of Allah (SWT) and the benefit of the Ummah.

This project is built with love and dedication, hoping it serves as a Sadaqah Jariyah for all those involved in its creation and maintenance.
