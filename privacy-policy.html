Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Information Collection

At Nurul Quran, we believe in complete privacy. We do not collect any personal information from our users. You can use all features of the application without creating an account or providing an email address.

Data Storage

Any data you save, such as bookmarks or settings, is stored locally on your device using browser technology (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third party.

Third-Party APIs

We use trusted third-party APIs to provide Quranic content and translations, such as alquran.cloud and islamic.network. These services may receive your IP address as part of the standard web request process, but we do not share any other data with them.

No Ads & No Tracking

Nurul Quran is a non-profit project. We do not display advertisements, we do not use tracking cookies, and we do not sell your data. Our only goal is to provide a clean and focused environment for spiritual growth.

Safety for All Ages

Our content is suitable for all ages. We ensure that all information provided is accurate and respectful, making it a safe space for children and adults alike.
